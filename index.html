Open Data Portal: Immediate Access to Government Information

The new open data portal launched by the government offers citizens immediate and free access to a wide range of public information. This portal includes data on budgets, infrastructure projects, healthcare services, and much more. The goal is to promote transparency and enable citizens to monitor the government's actions, fostering greater participation and trust in public management.

How Data is Improving Urban Management

The use of Big Data is revolutionizing urban management, allowing local administrations to make more informed and effective decisions. By analyzing large volumes of data, cities can optimize services such as waste collection, traffic, and public safety. For example, traffic data analysis has enabled the reduction of travel times and improved air quality.

Intelligent Transport: Route Optimization Thanks to Data

Intelligent transport is transforming the way we move in cities. Thanks to the analysis of traffic data, authorities can optimize public transport routes, reduce waiting times, and improve the efficiency of the transport system. This approach is making cities more livable and sustainable. Sensors installed on the streets collect real-time data on traffic flow, allowing authorities to intervene quickly in case of congestion or accidents.
